# Capstone-Sales-Analysis-Power-Bi-
This project delivers a high-impact Power BI solution for Northwind Traders, transforming fragmented wholesale data into a centralized intelligence hub. The dashboard provides a 360-degree view of company operations, moving the organization from manual reporting to proactive, data-driven decision-making
🚀 Key Insights & Features
The report consolidates complex datasets into four critical analytical pillars:

Sales Analysis: Monitors revenue trends, profit margins, and growth metrics to identify high-performing markets.

Customer Segmentation: Profiles purchasing behaviors to drive targeted marketing and improve retention strategies.

Inventory Trends: Tracks stock movement and turnover rates to optimize supply chain efficiency and prevent stockouts.

Employee Performance: Evaluates sales representative productivity to recognize top talent and optimize regional coverage.

🛠️ Technical Execution
Data Modeling: Established a robust Star Schema to integrate multiple relational tables (Orders, Customers, Employees, and Products).

Advanced DAX: Authored custom measures for Time Intelligence (YTD/YoY growth), dynamic rankings, and KPI tracking.

Power Query: Performed extensive ETL processes to clean, shape, and normalize raw data for accurate reporting.

Interactive UX: Designed a user-centric interface featuring dynamic filters, drill-through capabilities, and intuitive navigation.

📈 Business Impact
This dashboard revolutionizes how Northwind Traders interacts with its data. By facilitating real-time exploration and highlighting actionable patterns, it empowers stakeholders to optimize inventory, enhance customer relationships, and maintain a competitive edge in the wholesale market.
